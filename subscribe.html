Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What is Parentech and when will it launch?
Parentech is a secure digital platform being built to connect parents, families, trusted professionals and organisations, in order to strengthen families and promote the best interests of every child. We are currently in development, and subscribers will be the first to know our official launch date.
How much does Parentech cost?
Family membership plans start from R49 a month for Essential, R79 a month for Premium, and R149 a month for Family Plus. Professional marketplace plans start from R299 a month. Subscribing now helps you lock in founding member pricing.
Is my personal information safe?
Yes. Parentech is designed to keep your family's information safe and private, and we handle personal information in line with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). We will never share your details without your consent.
Can professionals and organisations join too?
Absolutely. Lawyers, doctors, psychologists, social workers, tutors, financial advisors, schools, healthcare providers and insurance professionals can all join the Professional Marketplace. Schools, NGOs, government departments, corporates and faith-based organisations can also partner with us.
Do I need to pay anything to subscribe now?
No. Joining our early access waitlist is completely free. There is no payment required until Parentech officially launches and you choose to activate a paid membership plan.
What happens after I subscribe?
You will receive a confirmation, followed by regular updates as we build Parentech. Once we launch, founding subscribers will be given priority access and locked in pricing.
