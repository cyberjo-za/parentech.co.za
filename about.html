Who We Are

A digital ecosystem built around families

Parentech connects families to verified professionals, educational resources, legal guidance, financial tools and community organisations, all through one easy-to-use digital ecosystem.

We are a proudly South African company being built for South African families, whether you are a single parent, separated parent, co-parenting household, grandparent or guardian.

🌍

Africa's Digital Family Support Ecosystem

We are building one connected platform where parents, professionals, schools, government and community organisations can work together in the best interests of every child, across South Africa and, in time, the rest of Africa.

🔭

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading digital family support ecosystem, empowering millions of families through technology, collaboration and trusted partnerships.

🎯

Our Mission

To strengthen families by providing affordable, accessible and trusted support services, while promoting the best interests of every child.

Partnerships

The Parentech Family Impact Alliance

Parentech believes no organisation can solve family challenges alone. We are building an alliance with NGOs, schools, government, healthcare organisations, faith-based organisations, corporates and community organisations, so that families can access more support, in more places.

Together with our partners we aim to

  • Promote healthier communities
